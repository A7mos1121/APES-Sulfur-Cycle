Sulfur cycle

What is the Sulfur Cycle?

  • The Sulfur Cycle is the movement of sulfur through the atmosphere, minerals, and living things.

  • Sulfur is incredibly important because it contains many proteins that are needed to live.

  • Sulfur is mainly found within sedimentary rocks and the ocean.
